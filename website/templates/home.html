Acupuncture

There are roughly 365 acupoints in the human body. These acupoints are the positions where the meridians, collateral qi and blood gather and pass through the surface of the body. These points are reaction areas connected with the internal organs of the body. The practice of acupuncture needles are solid, very thin sterile stainless steel needle (usually only 0.2-0.3mm) and pine shaped for quick and painless insertion into the specific points through the body’s meridians which adjust the flow of energy in the body and function of the internal organs to achieve a therapeutic effect.

Modern research proves that acupuncture stimulates the release of endorphins and bioactive substances in the blood and central nervous system of the human body to improve blood circulation which quickly adjusts the human body’s functions. Acupuncture is beneficial for improving the body’s natural aging process, disease and injury recovery, inflammation and the body’s overall mental and physical well-being.

Therapeutic Benefits of Acupuncture

It is generally believed that acupuncture is only suitable for pain. In fact, it is also suitable for internal medicine, surgery, gynecology, pediatrics, facial features and other departments. Its therapeutic effect is relatively rapid. For example, if the spleen and stomach function is poor, the medicine cannot be fully absorbed and used, and the effect will be slow. If it is combined with acupuncture treatment, can speed up its curative effect, because acupuncture at acupoints can promote qi and blood through the meridians, regulate the organs, balance the body, and treat both the symptoms and the root causes, so the effect is more obvious.
Clinically, it is often used in menstrual disorders, dysmenorrhea, infertility, menopausal syndrome, nasal sensitivity, diarrhea, constipation, dizziness, headache, Migraine, insomnia, facial paralysis, stroke sequelae, acne, skin sensitivity, etc. It is also effective in the treatment of beauty and weight management. effect. In addition to stimulating body functions and improving disease resistance, acupuncture therapy is also effective in sedation and analgesia, and has preventive and therapeutic effects. Acupuncture treats diseases by dredging meridians, harmonizing organs, and moving qi and blood. It can also regulate the body while treating diseases. Acupuncture has very few side effects and is quite safe and reliable.

What is scientific Chinese medicine? Understand the production principles and five major characteristics of scientific Chinese medicine ​ Scientific Chinese medicine was originally called "concentrated Chinese medicine preparations". It is a nickname derived from modern markets and pharmaceutical marketing strategies. It is also a modern preparation developed for the busy life of today's society. It mainly processes Chinese medicinal materials through cleaning, processing, extraction, concentration, and spray granulation together with excipients. According to the pharmaceutical factory regulations of the Department of Traditional Chinese Medicine of the Ministry of Health and Welfare of Taiwan, concentrated traditional Chinese medicine preparations must be produced by traditional Chinese medicine factories certified by GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice). Its safety specifications include:
(1) manufacturing process control
(2) high quality and stability
(3) heavy metal residue testing
(4) pesticide residue testing
(5) microbial content testing and other key items. Scientific Chinese medicine has the advantages of high safety, easy preparation, and portability.
